Mr. Yukihiro Matsumoto, Matz serves as Chief Architect of Ruby at Heroku, Inc. Mr. Matsumoto is a self-taught computer programmer. He serves as a Member of Advisory Board at Rhomobile, Inc. He is a research fellow of the Network Applied Communication Laboratory, an open source systems integrator company in Shimane Prefecture, Japan. He is also a fellow at Rakuten Institute of Technology, an R&D organization affiliated with Rakuten, Japan's largest e-commerce company. He is the creator of the free and open source Ruby programming language. He was a member of Ikuo Nakata's research lab on programming languages and compilers. Mr. Matsumoto graduated with a degree in information science from Tsukuba University.
