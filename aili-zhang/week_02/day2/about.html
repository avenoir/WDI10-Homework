About Larry Wall

Larry Wall (born September 27, 1954) is a computer programmer and author, most widely known as the creator of the Perl programming language. Wall grew up in Los Angeles and then Bremerton, Washington, before starting higher education at Seattle Pacific University in 1976, majoring in chemistry and music and later pre-medicine with a hiatus of several years working in the university's computing center before graduating with a bachelor's degree in Natural and Artificial Languages.
